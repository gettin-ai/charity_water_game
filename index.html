how to play

Clean The Bottle

  1. Match 4 blobs of the same color to clear them.
  2. Clean and dirty blobs can clear together when 4 or more of the same color connect.
  3. Click yellow cans for bonus points and extra purification.
  4. Click toxic sludge to reduce the penalty before it times out.
  5. Win by clearing every dirty blob before the bottle fills up.
